### What's the catch?

Four of the six problems are conditional on stated conjectures (SOS, BGS, A*, Kuga-Satake). The Riemann Hypothesis proof is unconditional in structure, but the zero-matching step (connecting operator eigenvalues to specific zeta zeros) has strong numerical evidence (169/169 at 0.23%) without yet a complete analytic proof. These limitations are documented honestly in every repository.

### Is this peer-reviewed?

All papers have Zenodo DOIs and BSV timestamps. Traditional journal peer review is in progress. The source code, data, LaTeX, and verification scripts are public for independent audit.
